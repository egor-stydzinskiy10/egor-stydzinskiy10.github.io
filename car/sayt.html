Welcome to car site!

A car is a self-propelled wheeled vehicle that is driven by an engine installed in it and is intended for transporting people, cargo, towing vehicles, performing special works and transporting special equipment on trackless roads. It moves mainly over land.

In 1886, Karl Benz patented the first car.

In 1891, René Panard and Emile Levassor created the first "horseless" carriage, powered by a Daimler engine.

In 1901, Ransom Eli Olds established mass production of the Oldsmobile car in the USA.

In 1906, the first Grand Prix car race took place in Le Mans (France).

In 1908, Henry Ford launched the Ford-T car.

In 1934, Citroen Andre Gustave developed and established the production of a front-wheel drive car.

In 1979, an exhaust gas catalyst was invented, which made it possible to reduce emissions of harmful substances into the atmosphere.
